How to Play 🧬

1

Align the Known Fish: Drag each fish's DNA strip left or right. The ends of the DNA should align vertically. When they do, the status light turns GREEN!

2

Look at the Colors: DNA has 4 bases: A, T, C, and G. Try to align matching colors in the same columns!

3

Find the Mystery Fish: Drag the purple Mystery Fish DNA. Move it until it matches one of the fish perfectly. Look for matching vertical letters!

4

Solve the Case: Once the Mystery DNA matches 100%, guess the fish species and read amazing fun facts about it!
